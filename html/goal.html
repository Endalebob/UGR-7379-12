goal


I have a plan for my life a to live as God intended. First of all, to do anything one must know why he or she does that thing. In order to know why he or she does something, they have to know who they are. After knowing who they are, they know what to do. After knowing who I am, I decided to live as my creator wants me to live. My plan is to live a life that glorifies God.


As a student, my plan is to bring something new that can change the life of many people. And contributing my own part to change the status of our country.


Personally, I want to become truly humble and love another. I want to help those who need my help. Generally, I have short-term, and long-term plans.
